Our Vision & Mission

Vision

To become the leading digital-based angkringan that bridges technology and traditional Indonesian cuisine while fostering a vibrant community of food and tech enthusiasts.

Mission

  • To preserve the cultural heritage of angkringan while integrating modern technology
  • To create an inclusive community space where technology and culinary traditions coexist
  • To provide affordable, high-quality food experiences enhanced by digital innovation
  • To support local ingredients and sustainable practices through smart supply chain solutions
  • To continuously evolve our concept based on community feedback and technological advancements
